About Me

Hello! My name is Jared Pacatan, and I am a passionate web developer and technology enthusiast. I created this website to share my projects, experiences, and journey in the field of software development.

My interest in technology began during high school when I started experimenting with website design and basic programming. Since then, I have continued learning modern web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various development frameworks.

Throughout my education and personal projects, I have developed skills in front-end development, problem-solving, and user experience design. I enjoy building websites that are both visually appealing and easy to use.

Beyond coding, I enjoy reading, photography, traveling, and staying active through sports and outdoor activities. These interests help me stay creative and bring fresh perspectives to my work.

My goal is to continue growing as a developer, contribute to meaningful projects, and use technology to create solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives.
